Training

At Birzeit Equestrian Club, we offer a variety of training programs to help riders develop their skills and knowledge. Our experienced trainers are dedicated to providing a safe and enjoyable environment for all members. Some of our popular training options include:

Beginner lessons: Suitable for riders with no prior experience, these lessons will help you learn the basics of equestrianism.

Intermediate lessons: Designed for riders who have some experience, these lessons will focus on refining your skills and honing your technique.

Advanced lessons: For experienced riders, these lessons will challenge you to push your limits and develop new skills.

Horse care and training: Our trainers will provide you with the knowledge and skills needed to care for and train your horse, ensuring that you have a happy and healthy relationship with your mount.

Specialty classes: We offer a range of specialized classes, such as dressage, show jumping, and eventing, to cater to the unique needs of our members.

To schedule a training session, please contact our club at the information provided on our main page.
